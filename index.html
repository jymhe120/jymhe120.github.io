Buy Online Shop in Store (BOPS)

The data of this project is provided by a Zale's, a jewelry Retail chain. The store is interested in figuring out the impact of Buy-Online-Pickup-In Store (BOPS) strategies on sales and returns on store level, consumer level, and product level.

As the business analysts working on this project, we are using the data to construct serveral to answer the following questions:

  • What is the impact of implementing BOPS strategy on online channel sales?
  • What is the impact of implementing BOPS strategy on online channel returns?
  • What is the impact of using the BOPS service on online customer purchase behavior?
  • What is the impact of using the BOPS service on online customer return behavior?
  • What is the impact of implementing BOPS strategy on product-level sales and returns?
  • How does the impact of implementing BOPS strategy vary across product categories?

    The data comes from Austin Anmial Shelter (Kaggle Data Challenge). The dataset features cats and dogs that are taken in by the Animal Shelter, their breed, color, age, and the eventual outcome of these animals. This analysis focuses on finding out what features contribute the higher adoption rate for these sheltered animals.

    HOME MORTGAGE DISCLOUSRE ACT

    The U.S Nationa Mortgage dataset contains 11M+ records that include applications for home purchase, home improvement, and refinancing. The objective of this project is to build a machine learning classification model that would be trained on historical data and make predictions on loan approval. In addition, this data analysis addresses the underlying idea of machine bias in current decision support sysmtems that uses machine learning and artificial intelligence.
